Architecture

The Taj Mahal is a remarkable blend of various architectural styles,including mugal ,persian and Indian. It is made of white marble, which was brought from diffrent parts of India and Central Asia.

The mausoleum os known for its symmetrical design, intricate carving and the iconic central dome. The surrounding gardens and the Yamuna river add to its beauty, making it a masterpiece of Islamic architecture.
